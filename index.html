Not long ago Nando’s and PERI-PERI sauce were the only things that sprang to mind when ‘spicy chicken’ was done in Britain. At Voodoo Chicken we shook things up a bit and started doing things a little differently.

We loved Nando’s. Growing up next to their first branch, I considered a trip to Nando’s as the biggest treat imaginable. When our Portuguese family friends would join they’d charm the waiters in their native tongue and receive secret off-menu treats in return. It was so exciting and I still have crystal clear memories of it today. It brought family, friends, and even staff together. But as we grew up, so did Nando’s, and gradually we saw our beloved family outing lose its local charm and change into an easy clean, fast in, fast out experience. So I asked myself, why can’t I bring back the destination that we loved and the cherished feelings it created?

During my career in the food industry, working everywhere from farms to street food markets, I fell in love with the flavour and spices of cajun chicken. So I started teaming up with chefs to create authentic recipes, constantly looking to the communities of rural Louisiana for inspiration and recreating their wonderful food over here. The flavours we created and feeling it provides is so good that we know everyone has to experience it.

WE DELIVER HOSPITALITY FROM THE HEART TO CREATE PLACES WHERE PEOPLE - STAFF AND CUSTOMERS - FEEL THEY BELONG.

THE EXPERIENCE:
We create customer experiences that are characterised by enjoyment, by making people feel recognised, helping them feel comfortable and, above all, making them feel special.

THE ENVIRONMENT:
The environments we create are theatrical and full of atmosphere. We serve classic food and provide a versatile setting that adapts to the specific desires of each customer.

HOSPITALITY:
We offer a unique style of hospitality - one that demands a sense of respect towards each other and the customer, a sense of empathy and the anticipation of needs before they are expressed, always delivered with warmth.

INTERNAL CULTURE:
We foster a strong sense of family, towards each other and towards the company. We take pride in what we do and we all share a sense of responsibility, right across the organisation. We always maintain a sense of humility about our achievements.

PEOPLE:
Our leadership aims to inspire, to be involved at all times in all aspects of the business and to continue to invest with generosity in its people, who treat each other with care and respect and are always fair in their dealings with one another.

PRACTICE:
In all that we do, we act with authority and behave with integrity. We seek to deliver consistently, with meticulous attention to detail. As an organisation, we never stand still and aim for continuous progression.

We’ve worked hard to match the flavours of rural Louisiana with ingredients we can get hold of to create an authentic menu with ingredients sourced as locally as possible, or grown and transported with care for the environment. We try to balance healthy eating with hearty eating, and wherever we can we source our supplies from small family run businesses that make the best product we can buy.
